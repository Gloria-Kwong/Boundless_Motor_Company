What is Perpetual Motion and How does it works

Have you seen a movie called "Snowpiercer"?

At the end of the day, when resources are ran out, do we still have the opportunity to board the Noah's Ark with perpetual motion to find the last chance to live? No one has a definite answer. Even in the day of real resource exhaustion, whether we can get a "Noah's ark" is unknown.

So the design concept of our automobiles comes from this movie similar to Noah's Ark train, in today's era of population explosion and resource overload consumption, we want to create a car with perpetual motion to break the rules, perpetual motion is the core of our product.

What is perpetual motion?

A perpetual motion machine is a machine that does not require external energy input or only initial energy to do work forever. Therefore, the energy carried in our car from the factory is the sum of all the energy used in the car's lifetime.

How does it works?

The principle of operation of a perpetual motion machine is very simple. A perpetual motion machine is similar to an engine, the soul of a car. The difference is that a perpetual motion machine can carry energy. The energy stored in the perpetual motion machine that each car carries when it leaves the factory is all the energy that the car will use in its lifetime. With the addition of this energy, although the perpetual motion machine is an isolated system, it will continue to do work, forming a self-body cycle.

How perpetual motion cars are created

Work PROCEDURE

#1

Rare and natural sources will be collected by the company used for creating perpetual motion machines.

#2

Perpetual motion machines carry the collected energy and later transform it into infinite source stored in our warehouse.

#3

The machines keep doing work with initial energy as an innate heat source and can be used in a variety of products.
